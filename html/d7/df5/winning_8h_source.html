4 * @brief Contains the function for checking if someone has won

6 * @details
7 * This file contains the declaration of the function used to check if a player has won

9
10 * # VICTORY ALGORITHM
11 * ## Vertical / Horizontal:
12 * The algorithm starts at the border and iterates to the other side. It goes in 2 different
13 * directions and counts on 2 axes. It runs in one of the directions till a cell is reached that
14 * is not marked by our player. Diagonal: The algorithm starts at the given cell coordinates. It
15 * goes in 4 different direction and counts on 2 axes. Every step in one of the directions
16 * increments the dedicated axis until a cell is reached that is not marked by our player.
17 *
18 * The return value is true when one axis or more axes has / have reached the win condition
19 * (line to the other side).
20 * ```text
21 * UP UP UP | axis | direction 1 | direction 2 |
22 * LEFT RIGHT |------------+-------------+-------------|
23 * \ | / | vertical | down | |
24 * LEFT ---+---> RIGHT | horizontal | right | |
25 * / | \ | diagonal_1 | up right | down left |
26 * DOWN v DOWN | diagonal_2 | up left | down right |
27 * LEFT DOWN RIGHT
28 * ```
